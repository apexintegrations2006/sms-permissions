Complete SMS Consent Flow

Our consent process follows a strict two-stage opt-in system that ensures customers explicitly consent before receiving any automated messages.

  1. Customer Information Collection

    A customer's name and phone number are added to our Detail Pro system in one of two ways:

    • In-Person: Staff manually adds customer information during service intake
    • Online Booking: Customer provides their information through our online booking system

    Note: Simply collecting a phone number does NOT grant permission to send messages.

  2. Staff Initiates Opt-In Process

    Before any messages can be sent, staff must manually click the "Send Opt-In Text" button in the Detail Pro app. This sends the initial opt-in message to the customer.

    üîí SYSTEM BLOCKS ALL MESSAGES

    At this stage, the system displays "Waiting for Customer to Opt In" and blocks staff from sending ANY messages until the customer responds with YES, START, or UNSTOP.

  3. Customer Receives Initial Opt-In Message

    The customer receives the following message (this is the ONLY message sent before consent):

    Hi [Customer Name], this is Detail Pro. Reply YES, START, or UNSTOP to receive appointment reminders and messages. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time.

    Key Point: This message clearly explains what the customer is consenting to and provides opt-out instructions upfront.

  4. Customer Actively Opts In

    The customer must reply with one of the following keywords to provide explicit consent:

    • YES
    • START
    • UNSTOP

    ‚ö†Ô∏è CONSENT IS REQUIRED

    Until the customer replies with one of these keywords, staff CANNOT send any messages‚Äîno appointment reminders, no service notifications, nothing. The system enforces this automatically.

  5. System Confirms Subscription

    Once the customer replies YES/START/UNSTOP, they immediately receive a confirmation message:

    Thank you! You are now subscribed to receive messages. Reply STOP to opt out at any time.

    ‚úÖ CONSENT GRANTED - MESSAGING ENABLED

    Only after this confirmation can staff send appointment reminders, service completion notifications, and other transactional messages.

  6. Regular Transactional Messages

    With explicit consent obtained, customers now receive:

    • Appointment reminders
    • Service completion notifications
    • Scheduling links
    • Service follow-up messages

    All messages are transactional and service-related. We do not send promotional or marketing messages.

Opt-Out Process

Customer Replies STOP

Customers can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. When they do:

üîí COMPLETE MESSAGING BLOCK

All messaging is immediately and completely blocked. Staff cannot send ANY messages to this customer‚Äînot even a new opt-in message. The customer must initiate re-subscription themselves.

Customer receives automatic STOP confirmation from the network

Network STOP Response:

NETWORK MSG: You replied with the word "stop" which blocks all texts sent from this number. Text back "unstop" or "start" to receive messages again.

Re-Subscribing After STOP

If a customer wants to receive messages again after opting out, they must text UNSTOP or START back to our number. Staff cannot re-initiate messaging‚Äîonly the customer can unblock it.

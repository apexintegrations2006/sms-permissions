Our Consent Process

Apex Integrations LLC obtains consent from customers before sending SMS messages. We primarily use verbal consent confirmed and documented in our Detail Pro app.

How We Obtain Consent

During service booking or intake, our staff:

  1. Ask the customer verbally: Staff explain that we send text messages for appointment reminders, service updates, and scheduling. We ask the customer if they would like to receive these messages.
  2. Receive affirmative consent: The customer must say "yes" or otherwise clearly agree to receive SMS messages. Simply providing a phone number is not enough.
  3. Document consent in Detail Pro app: After receiving verbal consent, staff immediately record this by activating the SMS consent toggle in the Detail Pro app for that customer.

What customers are told:

"We can send you text messages for appointment reminders, service completion notifications, and scheduling links. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out. Is it okay if we text you at this number?"

Additional Consent Methods

In some cases, we may also obtain consent through:

  • Written forms: Customers may check a box or sign a form indicating SMS consent when available
  • Digital opt-in: Customers may provide consent through online booking forms or customer portals

However, verbal consent documented in the Detail Pro app is our primary method.

Types of Messages We Send

All messages are transactional and service-related. Customers receive:

  • Appointment Reminders: Notifications about upcoming scheduled appointments
  • Service Completion Notifications: Alerts when their vehicle is ready for pickup
  • Scheduling Links: Links to book or reschedule appointments
  • Service Follow-Ups: Post-service check-ins and feedback requests

We do not send marketing or promotional messages unless separately opted in.

How to Opt Out

Customers can stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message
  • Contacting us at Apexintegrations2006@gmail.com
  • Letting us know in person or by phone

Record Keeping

We maintain records of all SMS consent, including:

  • Customer name and phone number
  • Date consent was obtained

These records are securely stored and available for compliance review.
